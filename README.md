## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I connect with someone?
1. Tap the person icon
2. Share your unique code with your partner
3. Have them enter your code (or enter theirs)
4. You're connected!

### How do I send a pulse?
Simply tap the orb. Your person will feel it instantly.

### Can I send a message?
Long press the orb to choose from preset messages: "thinking of you," "good morning," "good night," "miss you," or "love you."

### How do I customize colors?
Go to Settings to adjust the orb and theme colors to your preference.

### Does the widget work?
Yes! Add the Eccoti widget to your home screen. It will glow when you receive a pulse.
